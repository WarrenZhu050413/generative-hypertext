🧱 Layout Anatomy▶

Component Stack

  • SelectionOverlay tracks ranges inside cards.
  • HypertextPalette positions near the selection and captures instructions.
  • HyperlinkToken renders pill based on state (loading, ready, error).
  • DestinationModal previews generated content when the pill is clicked.
🚀 Interaction Notes▶
  • From my own experience, the palette should auto-dismiss on Esc or when selection changes.
  • Looking at this differently, pressing the hotkey twice could toggle the last hyperlink, mirroring “quick edit.”
  • The problem is focus management; keep the palette inside a portal so Tab does not jump out of the card.
  • I am curious about adding a glanceable badge on cards showing the count of hypertext tokens.
